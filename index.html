Abstract

For InfoVis part of the course project we chose a huge dataset that gathers tourism-related data from all around the world, built by United Nations World Tourism Organization (UNWTO) and available on Knoema. We made the following two visualizations on top of that:
    1. Tourism expenditure over the years
    2. Inbound and outbound tourism balance

We created a simple dashboard, making them correlated. You can see it here.
